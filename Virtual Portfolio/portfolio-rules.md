# Virtual Portfolio Rules

These rules define how I will manage my virtual portfolio.

## Rule 1: Understand Before Buying

I must understand:

* What the company does
* How it makes money
* Its major risks
* Why I believe it may be valuable

## Rule 2: Write Before Buying

I must document my investment thesis before purchasing a stock.

## Rule 3: Record My Expectations

I must write down what I expect to happen.

## Rule 4: Separate Process From Outcome

A stock making money does not automatically mean the decision was good.

A stock losing money does not automatically mean the decision was bad.

I will evaluate decisions based on the quality of the reasoning and the information available at the time.

## Rule 5: Record My Mistakes

Mistakes are part of the project.

I will document:

* What I believed
* What actually happened
* What I misunderstood
* What I will change

## Rule 6: Do Not Chase Performance

I will not buy a stock solely because its price has recently increased.

## Rule 7: Maintain a Written Record

Every transaction must be documented.

## Rule 8: Review Regularly

I will review my portfolio at regular intervals and evaluate both performance and decision quality.
